Upcoming Events

Discover the exciting events happening at our bookstore! Join us for insightful talks, book launches, and community gatherings.

Book Launch: "The Last Chapter"

Join us for the launch of "The Last Chapter," a gripping mystery novel that will keep you on the edge of your seat. Meet the author and hear about the inspiration behind the book. Get your copy signed at the event!

Author Talk: "The Art of Storytelling"

Do you have a passion for storytelling? In this event, best-selling author Jane Doe will discuss the art of crafting compelling narratives. Learn tips and tricks for writing your own stories!

Community Book Swap

Bring a book and take a book! Join our community book swap event, where book lovers come together to exchange their favorite reads. It's a great way to discover new books and meet fellow book enthusiasts.

Children’s Story Hour

Bring your little ones for an afternoon of fun stories and interactive activities. Our children's librarian will read from some of our best-loved picture books, followed by arts and crafts time!

Book Club: "Classic Novels"

Join our monthly book club meeting where we discuss classic novels. This month's selection is "Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen. We’ll explore its themes and characters in a lively discussion.

Poetry Reading Night

Celebrate the beauty of poetry with us! Our poetry reading night will feature local poets reading their works. Whether you’re a poetry lover or new to the genre, come and enjoy an evening of creative expression.
